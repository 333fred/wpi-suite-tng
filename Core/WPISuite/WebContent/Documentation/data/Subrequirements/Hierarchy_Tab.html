Hierarchy Tab

There is a hierarchy tab to view all existing children/parents in the tree view.

  • Click on the "Hierarchy" tab in the tree view in the left panel
  • You will see all requirements for the project arranged such that they show which requirements have children
  • Parents will be listed with a folder icon to their left where you can open the folder to view its children
  • This view functions like the tree view where you may open up requirements to view and edit them
  • You may create requirements in this view by right clicking and choosing the "Create Requirement" option
  • The Hierarchy tab does not show iterations

Child and Parent requirements may be removed from this tab:
  • To remove children from a parent, right click the parent folder in the view and hover your mouse over the "Remove Children >" option and then choose the child you wish to remove
    • Click image to enlarge

    The Edit Filter child will be removed from the hierarchy

  • To remove a parent, right click on a child requirement that has an existing parent and choose the "Remove Parent" option
    • Click image to enlarge

    The Edit Filter child will no longer have a parent

You may add children to any requirement by dragging and dropping the requirement onto another one
  • To drag a requirement, hold left click on the requirement and drag it onto another requirement. Release left click to drop the requirement

    • You may not drag requirements to the deleted folder.
