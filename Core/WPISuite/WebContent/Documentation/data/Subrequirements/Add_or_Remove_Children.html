Add or Remove Children from a Requirement

Adding a child to a requirement means that the child depends on its parent's completion in order to be completed itself. Requirements may have multiple children.

Adding Children

  • Open a requirement that will have children
  • Navigate to the "Subrequirements" tab in the right panel of the requirement edit view
  • There is a list of existing requirements in the "Add to SubRequirement Hierarchy" section of this panel. Make sure the "Add Children" radio button is selected and then choose a requirement that you wish to add as a child so that it is highlighted in blue
  • Click the "Assign Child" button to add the child
  • The child will be added to the "Children:" list above

  • If you open the child requirement, it will have the parent assigned to it next to the "Parent:" label under the Subrequirements tab

    • Requirements with children may not be deleted if it has children.


Removing Children

  • Open a requirement that you want to remove existing children from
  • Navigate to the "Subrequirements" tab in the right panel of the requirement edit view
  • Click the child you wish to remove
  • Click the "Remove Children" button to remove the child
  • The child will be removed from the "Children" list and placed back in the box below
