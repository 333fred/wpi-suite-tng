Closing Tabs

Tabs that have been opened can be closed by clicking the small "x" in the actual tab or by right clicking the tab and choosing the "close" option.

  • you may close all tabs by right clicking on any tab you have opened and choosing the "Close All" option
  • If you want to keep one tab open, but no longer want other tabs open, you can right click on the tab you want to keep and choose the "Close Others" option

Since the Requirements tab may not be closed, you can right click on that tab and choose the "Close Others" option to close all opened tabs.
