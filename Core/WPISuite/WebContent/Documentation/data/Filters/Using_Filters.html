Using Filters

Once you create a filter (refer to for details), you may use them to separate requirements in the table view

    Click the "Filters" tab in the tree view if you haven't already

    Enabling Filters

  • To enable a filter, click on the filter you wish to apply so that it is highlighted in blue. Click the "Enable" button below the list of filters to apply the filter. The filter will then bold and you will see in the table view that your filter only displays the Requirements specified by the filter
  • Multiple filters may be applied at once by holding CTRL and clicking the filters you wish to apply. Click "Enable" after the filters are selected


    • Disabling Filters

  • To disable a filter, select an active filter and click the "Disable" button located below the list of filters. The filter will be grayed out and inactive
  • Multiple filters may be disabled at once by holding CTRL and selecting a list of active filters. Click "Disable" after the filters are selected


    • Deleting Filters

  • To delete a filter, select any filter you wish to delete and click the "Delete" button below the list of filters
  • Multiple filters may be deleted by holding CTRL and selecting a list of filters you wish to delete. Click "Delete" after the filters are selected


    • Requirement fields can be rearranged by clicking on a header and dragging it to a different spot on the table view.
