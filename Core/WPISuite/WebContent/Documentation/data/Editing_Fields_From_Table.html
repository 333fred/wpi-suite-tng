Editing Fields from Table View

You may want to quickly edit fields for requirements in the table view without having to open each individual Requirement in edit view.

  • Click the "Enable Editing" button located on the bottom of the Requirements tab
  • You may double click on the field for any Requirement that you want to edit
  • Some fields have a drop down menu to choose options
  • You may click off of the field to deselect or choose another Requirement to edit by repeating the last two steps
  • To save your changes, simply click the "Save Changes" at the bottom of the table view
  • To discard your changes, click the "Discard Changes" button located at the bottom of the table view

Fields you may not edit will be grayed out and fields that have been edited will be highlighted in yellow.
