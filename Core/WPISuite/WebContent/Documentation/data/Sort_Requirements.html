Sorting Requirements in Table View

You may want sort the requirements in the table view in alphabetical/reverse alphabetical order.

  • You may click the headers above the list of requirements to sort them alphabetically or in reverse alphabetical order
    • When an arrow pointing upward is displayed, the requirements are being sorted alphabetically for that field
    • When an arrow pointing downward is displayed, the requirements are being sorted in reverse alphabetical order for that field

    The Iteration field is sorted by date. An up arrow shows requirements in the backlog first, followed by requirements sorted by the oldest iteration to the youngest iteration, then requirements in the deleted folder. A down arrow displays the reverse order of what was previously stated.

  • Requirement fields can be rearranged by clicking on a header and dragging it to a different spot on the table view
