Creating an Iteration

To create an iteration:

  • Click "Requirements Manager" a tab at the top of the Janeway client
  • There are multiple ways to create an iteration. click the "Create Iteration" button in the Home panel. You may also right click the root of the tree view labeled "Iterations" and click "New Iteration" or Right click the white space in the tree view and click "New Iteration"
  • Fill in the required fields
    • Name* - The name of the iteration
    • Start Date* - The starting date of the iteration
    • End Date* - The ending date of the iteration
    • The end date must be later than the start date, and the time segment cannot overlap with other iterations
  • Click "Save Iteration" to continue or exit by clicking "x" on the tab.
  • The iteration should be viewable in the Requirements tree view. If not, click refresh.
