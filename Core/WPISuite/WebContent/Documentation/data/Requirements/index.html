Requirements

Requirements are documented needs that a final product must contain and use.

  • Requirements have a number of attributes, including name, description, type, priority, status, iteration, estmated work, actual work, and release number.
  • Requirements can contain notes which are bits of text that relate to the requirement.
  • Requirements have a log that shows any changes made to the requirement, when the change was made, and who made the requirement.
  • Requirements have a list of users assigned to them and are worked on as requested.
