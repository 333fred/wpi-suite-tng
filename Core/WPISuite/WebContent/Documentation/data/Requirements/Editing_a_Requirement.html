Editing a Requirement

To edit a requirement:

  • Click "Requirements Manager" a tab at the top of the Janeway client
  • Select the requirement to edit in the Requirements table view or tree view
    • If no requirements appear, try clicking the "Refresh" button in the Requirements toolbar
  • With sufficient permissions, you may edit the requirement by double clicking the requirement or by pressing enter once the requirement is highlighted. You may open multiple requirements by holding Control and clicking the requirements you want to open or by holding down the left click and dragging over the requirements you wish to open
  • Altering a requirement should be similar to adding a requirement. See "Adding a Requirement" for details on the various fields.
