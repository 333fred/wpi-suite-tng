Deleting a requirement

To delete a requirement:

  • Click "Requirements Manager" a tab at the top
  • The requirement must have a new or complete status
  • Open the requirement by double clicking the requirement in the tree view or table view
  • Click on the dropdown menu labeled 'Status:' and choose the 'delete' option
  • Click the save requirement button
The requirement will now be moved to the 'deleted' folder. The fields for this requirement may not be edited until it is reassigned to an open status.
