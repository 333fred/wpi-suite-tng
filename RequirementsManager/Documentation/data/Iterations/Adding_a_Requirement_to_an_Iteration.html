Adding a Requirement to an Iteration

To add a requirement to an existing iteration, do one of the following:

  • Edit the requirement and select the iteration from the dropdown menu labeled 'Iteration:' and choose the appropriate iteration.
  • You may also drag a requirement to the desired iteration in the requirements list tab on the left hand side of the screen by clicking on the desired requirement and dragging it into an iteration. You may only drag a requirement into an iteration that has not been completed.
