Editing Multiple Estimates

You may want to edit multiple Estimates from the table view without having to open each individual Requirement in edit view.

  • Click the "Enable Editing" button located on the bottom of the Requirements tab
  • You may double click on the Estimate field for any Requirement that has a status that is Open or New
  • Type the new Estimate that the Requirement should have
  • You may click off of the field to deselect or choose another Requirement to edit by repeating the last two steps
  • To save your changes, simply click the "Save Changes" at the bottom of the table view
  • To discard your changes, click the "Discard Changes" button located at the bottom of the table view
