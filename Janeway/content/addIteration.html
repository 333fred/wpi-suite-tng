Creating an Iteration

To create an iteration
	- Click "Requirements Manager" a tab at the top
	- Click "Create Iteration" a button at the top left in the Home toolbar
	- Fill in the required fields
		- Name* - The name of the iteration
		- Start Date* - The starting date of the iteration
		- End Date* - The ending date of the iteration
			The end date must be later than the start date, and the time segment cannot overlap with other iterations
	- Click "Save Iteration" to continue or exit by clicking "x" on the tab
	- The iteration should be viewable in the Requirements List tab.  If not, click refresh
