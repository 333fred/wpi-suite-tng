Creating an Iteration

To create an iteration

  1. Click "Requirements Manager" a tab at the top
  2. Click "Create Iteration" a button at the top left in the Home toolbar
  3. Fill in the required fields
    • Name* - The name of the iteration
    • Start Date* - The starting date of the iteration
    • End Date* - The ending date of the iteration
    • The end date must be later than the start date, and the time segment cannot overlap with other iterations
  4. Click "Save Iteration" to continue or exit by clicking "x" on the tab.
  5. The iteration should be viewable in the Requirements List tab. If not, click refresh.
