Editing a Requirement

To edit a requirement

  1. Click "Requirements Manager" a tab at the top
  2. Select the requirement to edit in the Requirements List tab
    • If no requirements appear, try clicking the "Refresh" button in the Requirements toolbar
  3. With sufficient permisions, you may edit the requirement by clicking the "Edit Requirements" button (Alternatively, you may double click a requirement)
  4. Altering a requirement should be similar to adding a requirement. See "Adding a Requirement" for details.
