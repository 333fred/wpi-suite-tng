Editing a Iteration

To edit an iteration:

  1. Click "Requirements Manager" a tab at the top
  2. Select an iteration you wish to edit in the requirements tree view, located on the left side.
  3. Right click and select "Edit Iteration"
  4. Edit the name and/or date fields to reflect the updated information, making sure that all requirements of an iteration are still met
  5. Click the save button at the bottom of the page
